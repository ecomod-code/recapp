# Running a Quiz <Badge type="tip" text="Teachers" />

## Starting the quiz

When your questions are ready, click **Start quiz** in the Quiz questions tab, the Statistical evaluation tab, or via the play button on the dashboard.

The quiz state changes from <Badge type="warning" text="EDITING" /> to <Badge type="tip" text="STARTED" />. From this point:
- Students currently viewing the quiz automatically switch to the answering interface
- No further editing of questions or settings is possible while the quiz is running
- The **Statistical evaluation** tab appears as soon as the first answer is submitted

::: tip Sharing at the last moment
If students haven't joined yet, share the link or QR code now — students who open a started quiz land directly on the first question.
:::

## During the quiz

While the quiz is running you can:
- Watch the **Statistical evaluation** tab update in real time as answers come in
- See how many students have registered and how many are currently answering
- Switch to **Presentation mode** (toggle at the top-left of the Statistical evaluation tab) to project an enlarged results view for the class

Students work through questions at their own pace. After submitting their last answer, they see their individual results and the overall statistics — if you've enabled this in the [quiz settings](./create-quiz#student-permissions).

## Stopping the quiz

Click **Stop quiz** to end the answering phase. The quiz state changes to <Badge type="danger" text="STOPPED" />.

In stopped mode:
- No new answers can be submitted
- Everyone can still view the questions and statistics
- This is a natural moment to discuss the results as a group

You can switch back to editing mode at any time, but this **resets all statistics**. Stay in stopped mode if you want to review or export the results later.

## Exporting results

In the **Statistical evaluation** tab, two export options are available as CSV or PDF:

| Export | Contains |
|---|---|
| **Export quiz statistics** | Questions, question types, participant count, correct answer count per question |
| **Export question statistics** | Everything above, plus the distribution of answers across individual answer options for each question |
