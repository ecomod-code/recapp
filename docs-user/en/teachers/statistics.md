# Statistics <Badge type="tip" text="Teachers" />

The **Statistical evaluation** tab becomes available as soon as the quiz is started and at least one answer has been submitted. It updates automatically while the quiz is running.

## Overview

The overview lists all questions. Each question has a horizontal bar showing the proportion of correct and wrong answers:
- **Left half** — correct answers, with a tick mark and the absolute count
- **Right half** — wrong answers, with a cross and the absolute count

Students see this same view after completing the quiz — if you've enabled **Participants can view statistics** in the quiz settings. Below each question they also see whether their own answer was correct.

## Question detail

Click on any question text to open its detailed statistics:
- How many participants skipped the question
- Total correct and wrong answers
- For each answer option: how many times it was selected (absolute and relative), and whether it was the correct answer

Students also see which option they personally selected, indicated with a "You:" label next to their choice.

Use the **Back to Quiz** and **Next question** buttons at the bottom to navigate between questions.

## Presentation mode

Toggle **Presentation mode** at the top left of the Statistical evaluation tab to switch to an enlarged display suitable for projecting in class. Use this when walking through results with a group.

## Accessibility

The colour scheme in the statistics view is set per user via **Account settings → Colour palette**. Two palettes are available — red/green and blue/yellow — to accommodate different types of colour vision. Remind students they can adjust this in their own account settings.

## Resetting statistics

Statistics reset automatically when the quiz is returned to editing mode. To reset them without changing the quiz state, click **Reset statistics** in the top-right corner of the Statistical evaluation tab. This option is only available in stopped mode.

## Exporting

Export options are available at the top of the Statistical evaluation tab:

| Export | Contents |
|---|---|
| **Export quiz statistics** | Questions, question types, participant count, correct answer count |
| **Export question statistics** | All of the above, plus per-question answer distribution across all options |

Both are available as CSV and PDF.
