# Answering a Quiz <Badge type="info" text="Students" />

## When the quiz starts

When your teacher starts the quiz, your view automatically switches to the answering interface. If you're not already in the quiz, open the link or QR code — you'll land directly on the first question.

Questions are presented one at a time. Depending on the quiz settings, the order may be different for each student.

## Answering questions

**Single-choice questions** — Select one answer option.

**Multiple-choice questions** — Select all options you think are correct.

**Free-text questions** — Type your answer in the text field.

After selecting your answer, click **Submit** to confirm and move to the next question. Submitted answers cannot be changed. Work through all questions at your own pace.

## After completing the quiz

When you've answered all questions, you'll see your results — if your teacher has enabled this.

**Individual results** — For each question, you can see whether your answer was correct or incorrect, along with your specific selection shown next to the answer options.

**Overall statistics** — An overview of all questions showing how the whole group answered: the proportion of correct and wrong answers, and the count for each.

If no statistics are visible, your teacher has not enabled them for this quiz.

## Comments

If your teacher has enabled comments, you can leave a comment on the question currently on screen. Click **New comment** in the comment panel on the right.

- You can upvote comments from others using the thumbs-up icon
- You can delete your own comments
- Your teacher may mark comments as acknowledged (green) or dismiss them (grey) — dismissed comments are no longer visible to students
- If a comment has a **?** icon next to it, it relates to a specific question — click the icon to jump to that question

A comment with no **?** is a general comment about the quiz, not a specific question.
