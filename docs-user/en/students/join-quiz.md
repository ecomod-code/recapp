# Joining a Quiz <Badge type="info" text="Students" />

## How to join

Your teacher will share a **link** or **QR code** with you. Click the link or scan the QR code to open the quiz.

You have two options:

**With your university account** — Click **Login** and sign in with your standard university credentials. The quiz appears in your dashboard and you can return to it at any time from any device.

**Without logging in** — You can join without a university account. Recapp creates a temporary session for you. Be aware that this is tied to your current browser — if you close the tab or switch devices, you may not be able to continue where you left off.

## What to expect when you join

What you see depends on the current quiz state:

- <Badge type="warning" text="EDITING" /> — If the teacher has allowed it, you can submit questions. See [Creating Questions](./create-questions).
- <Badge type="tip" text="STARTED" /> — You go directly to the answering interface. See [Answering a Quiz](./answer-quiz).
- <Badge type="danger" text="STOPPED" /> — The quiz is no longer accepting answers. You can view the questions and, if permitted, the statistics.

## The dashboard

After logging in, the **dashboard** shows all quizzes you're connected to. Each card shows the title, participant and question counts, the teacher(s), and the current quiz status.

From the dashboard you can return to any active quiz at any time.

## Account settings

Click the circle with your name initials in the top-right corner to open your account settings.

| Setting | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Pseudonym** | A display name to use instead of your real name. Set this before joining if you want to participate pseudonymously. |
| **Participation options** | Your default name type: anonymous, pseudonym, or real name. |
| **Language** | Switch between German and English at any time. |
| **Colour palette** | Choose a colour scheme for accessibility (red/green or blue/yellow). |
